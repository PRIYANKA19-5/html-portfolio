Best poems I have read

My most favourite poems of all times:

The Road Not Taken

This is a narrative poem by Robert Frost which offers profound perception of decision making.

Sonnet 18

Written by William Shakespeare, this poems reflects the stability of love and its power to immortalize someone.

Gitanjali

Gitanjali is a series of 103 poems penned by Rabindranath Tagore about the end journey of man to God. It is one of the finest sources of old Indian modern poetry.
